Who are we?

We are a group of people of 6 members who noticed a problem in the society, people want to volunteer and don't know where to go, people and organizations need volunteers and can't get them easily.
We find the solution very simple, Give For Hope, A website that gives volunteers a place to help, getting two nationalities together to volunteer and help the community, and offering volunteers for places who need volunteers .
